Espresso

coffee, beverage brewed from the roasted and ground seeds of the tropical evergreen coffee plants of African origin. Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee served in small, strong shots and is the base for many coffee drinks. It's made from the same beans as coffee but is stronger, thicker, and higher in caffeine. There are many different ways to make espresso; an espresso machine, a moka pot, a phin, and an aeropress.
