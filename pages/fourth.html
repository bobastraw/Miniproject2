Phin

It works similar to an espresso portafilter, except the water is passed through by gravity instead of pressure. Assemble phin with chamber on top of lid, and pour hot water through phin. This makes the coffee grounds expand to fill the holes. To brew, simply tamp coffee in the chamber, and leave tamper in the chamber. Pour water into the chamber.
